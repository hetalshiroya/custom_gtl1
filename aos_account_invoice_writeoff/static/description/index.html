Partial Write-off For Remaining Debt and Full Write-off Due to Bad Debt

Key Features :
- Partial Write-off For Remaining Amount Due
- Full Write-off due to Bad Debt
- Single Account
- Single and Multiple Invoice

 

 

1. How to do partial write-off for the remaining amount due

Select Invoice and click Full Reconcile Invoices
Select Write-Off Journal and Write-Off Account and then click Reconcile
Amount Due become 0 and invoice status become paid

2. How to do full write-off for bad debt

Select Invoice and click Full Renconcile Invoices
Select Write-Off Journal and Write-Off Account and then click Reconcile
Amount Due become 0 and invoice status become paid
