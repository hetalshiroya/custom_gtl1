Validate Payment Against Multiple Invoices

This App allows you to validate one payment against multiple invoices wtih different amount.It will cover the four dirrefernt possible scenarios.

  1. Received the payment from customer against multiple invoices with different amount
  2. Send the payment to customer for multiple refund invoices with different amount
  3. Send the payment to supplier for multiple invoices with different amount
  4. Received the payment from supplier against multiple refund invoices with different amount
